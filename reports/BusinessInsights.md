# Business Insights

## Executive Summary

An analysis of 10,194 order records from 2023–2026 was conducted to evaluate sales performance, profitability, customer behavior, product performance, and regional trends. The analysis identified key growth drivers, profitability challenges, and opportunities for business improvement.

## Key Findings

### 1. Technology is the Best-Performing Category

Technology generated the highest sales revenue and total profit among all product categories. It also achieved the highest profit margin of 17.45%, making it the company's strongest-performing category.

### 2. Furniture is the Least Profitable Category

Despite generating significant sales revenue, Furniture achieved a profit margin of only 2.61%, substantially lower than Technology and Office Supplies.

### 3. Tables are Driving Furniture Losses

Within the Furniture category, Tables recorded a negative profit margin of approximately -8%, making them the primary contributor to poor category profitability.

### 4. High Discounts are Associated with Lower Profitability

Analysis showed a clear inverse relationship between discounts and profit. As discount levels increased, average profitability declined.

### 5. Discounts Above 30% Frequently Result in Losses

Furniture products receiving discounts of approximately 30% or higher consistently generated negative profits, suggesting that the current discount strategy may be unsustainable.

### 6. Highest Revenue Customer is Not the Most Valuable Customer

Sean Miller generated the highest sales revenue (approximately 25,043) but produced negative overall profit, demonstrating that revenue alone is not an indicator of customer value.

### 7. Profitability Should Be Used to Measure Customer Value

Customers such as Tamara Chand, Raymond Buch, and Sanjit Chand generated the highest profits and therefore represent some of the company's most valuable customers.

### 8. Business Performance Improved Over Time

Total sales increased from approximately 494K in 2023 to 746K in 2026. Profit also increased consistently each year, indicating strong long-term business growth.

### 9. Profit Increased Even During Temporary Revenue Decline

Although sales declined slightly in 2024, profitability increased compared to 2023, suggesting improvements in operational efficiency, pricing strategy, or product mix.

### 10. West Region is the Strongest Market

The West region generated the highest sales, highest profit, and highest profit margin (14.98%), making it the company's best-performing geographic market.

### 11. Central Region Requires Attention

The Central region generated higher sales than the South region but produced lower profit and a significantly lower profit margin (7.92%). This indicates potential inefficiencies that require further investigation.

## Recommendations

1. Review discounting policies for Furniture products, particularly Tables.
2. Investigate the causes of losses associated with high-revenue but low-profit customers.
3. Expand successful strategies currently used in the West region.
4. Analyze operational and pricing challenges within the Central region.
5. Focus marketing and retention efforts on highly profitable customer segments.
